Dark Art

In 2018 I started dabbling and experimenting with synthwave. Dark Art is my first attempt at creating a full length synthwave album. The tracks are inspired by Sega Megadrive soundtracks, mixed with some dark and atmospheric vibes (along the lines of John Carpenter).

During the creation of these tracks, there was no overthinking. If I liked the sound of it, I used it. I think the album is a good starting point and I aim to experiment with synths a lot more.

You can download the album for free on my Bandcamp page. The tracks are also available on Spotify and all major digital music stores.
