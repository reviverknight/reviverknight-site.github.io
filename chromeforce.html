ChromeForce is an NFT collection based on a military unit of Clone Soldiers. There are 200 animated characters available, all with different names, properties and traits. All of the characters have the electrifying 'boot up' animation and loop seamlessly. You can view the entire collection on my OpenSea page. Take a look at some of the characters below:

I created numerous different items and features (hair, tech, eyewear, eye colour, earrings .etc). These items were randomly compiled into short videos using After Effects Scripting.

The ChromeForce soldiers are a part of my 'Architect' universe. A cyberpunk/military themed project that sees the hero, Kane Kinetic trying to save the World from the corrupted 'Clone Horde'. Check out the video below to learn more about the story.
